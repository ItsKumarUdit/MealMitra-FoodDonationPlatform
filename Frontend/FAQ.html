Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate food?

Sign up on our platform, list your food items, and connect with requesters.

2. Who can request food?

Anyone in need, including individuals and NGOs, can request food donations.

3. Is there any cost for donating or receiving food?

No, our platform is completely free for both donors and requesters.

4. How do I ensure food safety while donating?

Provide well-packaged, fresh food and label it properly. Requesters should verify food quality before accepting.

5. Can restaurants or businesses donate surplus food?

Yes! Restaurants, caterers, and grocery stores can donate excess food to help reduce food waste.
